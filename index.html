About NSS

Mission & Vision

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is an Indian government-sponsored public service program conducted by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports of the Government of India. The scheme was launched in 1969 with the primary objective of developing the personality and character of the student youth through voluntary community service.

At CSIT College, our NSS unit is dedicated to instilling the idea of social welfare in students and providing service to society without bias. NSS volunteers work to ensure that everyone in society gets justice, freedom, and equality.

Goals of NSS

  • To understand the community in which they work
  • To identify the needs and problems of the community
  • To develop among themselves a sense of social and civic responsibility
  • To utilize their knowledge in finding practical solutions to community problems
  • To develop competence required for group-living and sharing of responsibilities
  • To gain skills in mobilizing community participation

Why Join NSS?

Our Activities

Swachhata Abhiyan

Cleanliness drives to promote hygiene and sanitation in college campus and surrounding areas.

Blood Donation Camps

Regular blood donation camps to support local hospitals and create awareness about blood donation.

Awareness Rallies

Organize rallies to spread awareness on social issues like education, health, and environment.

Plantation Drives

Plantation campaigns to increase green cover and promote environmental consciousness.

Health Check-up Camps

Free health check-up camps for students and local communities to promote healthcare awareness.

Workshops & Seminars

Educational workshops and seminars on various social and environmental topics.
